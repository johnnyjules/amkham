THIS PLACID PLACE

lood 015

I composed and produced the EP "This Placid Place" which was released on the label Light Of Other Days in December 2019. The record contains four tracks, including a remix by Berlin-based DJ and producer Philipp Otterbach. All tracks (except the remix) where mixed by me and mastered by Brandenburg Mastering in Amsterdam. Buy the vinyl record on Bandcamp, Juno Records, HHV etc. or stream it on your favourite platform. A review by zweikommasieben magazine is on their website.

MAKE SOME ROOM

electronic relief in switzerland

I was kindly invited to contribute a track to the compilation "Make Some Room: Electronic Relief in Switzerland", together with 130 other Swiss artists, including heavyweights like Yello, Bruno Spoerri or Thomas Fehlmann and many musicians and friends I admire. 100 percent of the proceeds go to the artists as support in times when clubs have to stay closed due COVID-19. Listen and buy the tune here. Read about the compilation here.

SCHWARZE SCHWEIZ

lux rec

I contributed a track to the compilation "Schwarze Schweiz" by Swiss label Lux Rec under my artist moniker AM Khamsaa. The record was released in October 2019. www.luxrec.net
